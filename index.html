About me

Got a minute of time? Let me introduce myself briefly.

Who I am

My name is Matti and I am a 30 years old web developer, currently working in the financial technology industry in London.

Where I come from

I come from Finland, the land of the midnight sun and cold arctic winters. Recently I have had my home also in South Korea.

What I do

Having graduated with a master's degree in Information Systems, my career has progressed towards web development.

Timeline

Ok, ready to see what I have done in my life until now?

  • 1985-2004

    I was born in 1985 in a small countryside village in Eastern Finland, where I lived with my parents and my older brother until I was 18 years old. The nearest town was 15 km away, but this didn't bother me as I had always plenty of things going on. My favourite hobbies included skiing, fishing and playing computer games. When I became 18, I did my mandatory military service in Finnish Lapland.

  • 2005-2008

    In 2005 I finally became independent and moved to the central part of Finland. I started my bachelor's studies at the University of Jyväskylä and chose Information Systems Science as my major subject. As a minor, I studied a number of courses in economics and business studies. However the most interesting courses were all related to web development, and I learned to develop my own dynamic websites with HTML, CSS, PHP and MySQL. Life was fun and at the same time I became increasingly interested in foreign cultures.

  • 2009-2012

    As soon as I received my bachelor's degree, I continued directly to master's studies in Information Systems. However I needed a break, so I signed up for a year of exchange studies in South Korea. This decision ended up changing my life, as I liked the country so much that I returned back to Korea in 2011 for a work opportunity as a junior market analyst. During those times I also met my better half and we decided to move to Finland, where I wrapped up my master's studies and worked in two different web content management positions.

  • 2013-2015

    In 2013 we moved to the United Kingdom and I was lucky enough to land a job at Lloyds Banking Group in London as a web content manager. My employment with the bank lasted for 2.5 years and during those times I learned to apply my technical knowledge to develop better online banking as a member of globally distributed agile teams. The years with LBG also teached me organisational skills and strengthened my plans of learning more and more about web development.

  • 2016-

    In February 2016 me and my wife will be moving back to South Korea to establish our family, as we are expecting our first child to be born in May. This will also provide an exciting challenge for my career and I'm very much looking forward to find the correct organisation to make the next step in Korea.
